    Stream Outline

    We plan to stream game reviews on YouTube for 3-5 hours. We'll start by preparing, then recording, have a break time (more if needed), continuing the recording, start editing, have another break time, continue editing, then finish streaming.

    We will also talk to our viewers while we stream; the topics we will talk about will focus on the video games we are playing.

    We do not predict that we will be changing our content in the middle of the stream, I think if we do that then it will be targeting a different audience and our viewers might lose interest.

    For interactions in the audience, we can livestream our gameplays and game reviews while also making a poll for the viewers that are watching the livestream to answer some fun questions.

    On the topic on what we will display for the duation of the stream, we will demonstrate how the game works and make it appealing for the viewers on our screen. We will also display various reviews regarding the game, such as how simple it is to play and how appropriate it is for all ages.

    Every hour, overlay changes might appear sloppy. To avoid boring the viewers, we will just employ one or two overlays to alter during the show.

    We will not have breaks when broadcasting because it is continuous, and we have already determined how long the stream will be.

    To avoid violating any Copyright acts; Perhaps we will ask one of the members in the group to create a video tutorial explaining the game‚Äôs mechanics. And we‚Äôll show it out during the stream. So yes, there might be a video presented while streaming.

    Promotional Plan

    Goals:

    Our objectives are to get the stream to a successful 5 star rating, to attain our target viewership, and to ensure that every viewer gains as much knowledge from the stream as possible.

    Target Audience:

    Video game enthusiasts, people interested in discovering new video game releases and competitive games interested in learning more about new strategies and techniques.

    Content:

    Video game reviews. Video game reviewers will provide an in-depth analysis of particular games, highlighting both their pro et contra. Video game playthroughs: video game reviewers will play a game from start to finish, providing commentary on their experience, highlighting any issues we encounter along the way.

    Platforms:

    Youtube; the website will have a YouTube channel dedicated to video game reviews and playthroughs and for streaming, where users can interact and engage with the reviewer.
